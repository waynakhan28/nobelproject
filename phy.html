2020 - Roger Penrose

Contribution: Black hole formation and general relativity

History: Demonstrated black hole formation as a robust prediction of general relativity. Theoretical physics exploration of black holes.

2020 - Reinhard Genzel & Andrea Ghez

Contribution: Discovery of supermassive black hole at Milky Way center

History: Observational proof of supermassive compact object at our galaxy's center.

2021 - Syukuro Manabe & Klaus Hasselmann

Contribution: Climate modeling & global warming prediction

History: Developed climate models quantifying variability; advanced climate science as a scientific discipline.

2021 - Giorgio Parisi

Contribution: Disorder and fluctuations in physical systems

History: Discovery of interplay of disorder and fluctuations from atomic to planetary scales in physical systems.

2022 - Alain Aspect, John F. Clauser, Anton Zeilinger

Contribution: Entangled photons experiments

History: Demonstrated violation of Bell inequalities, pioneering quantum information science.

2023 - Pierre Agostini, Ferenc Krausz, Anne L’Huillier

Contribution: Attosecond pulse generation

History: Developed methods generating attosecond pulses of light to study electron dynamics in matter.

2024 - John J. Hopfield & Geoffrey Hinton

Contribution: Artificial neural networks & machine learning

History: Foundational discoveries in neural networks; Hopfield networks and backpropagation for deep learning.

2025 - John Clarke, Michel H. Devoret, John M. Martinis

Contribution: Superconducting circuits demonstrating quantum phenomena

History: Demonstrated quantum mechanical properties at macroscopic scale, foundation for quantum computing and quantum technologies.
